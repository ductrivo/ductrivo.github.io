## Isère


### North-Isère: During a heatwave, CAPI announces the permanent closure of the Fallavier lake beach.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/76e2f285-3446-47af-b805-f8a10dea0fdf/920x517?webp=false"

In the northern part of Isère, during a heatwave, the Portes de l'Isère agglomeration community (CAPI) has made the final decision to permanently close the Fallavier lake beach, which has already been closed to swimming for a year. ### Heatwave in Isère: In Saint Clair de la Tour, the town hall is opening the church so that the population can find relief from the heat.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/346baaf0-2ef6-4660-9615-604e3b8d188e/920x517?webp=false"

The Isère region is currently experiencing a heatwave, classified as orange alert. ### Assaults on the face, parents in custody, investigation reclassified… What we know about the death of a 2-month-old baby in Creil


On February 1, 2023, a two-month-old baby died in Creil (Oise). The circumstances of his death were initially considered accidental. However, marks on the baby's face were found during an autopsy. As a result, the parents were placed under arrest and the investigation was reclassified as voluntary homicide or manslaughter. Authorities have not yet provided details on the nature of the injuries or the reasons for this reclassification. The investigation is ongoing.

### France-Norway: Why are the French forced to play in their green jerseys this Friday?


The France-Norway match, scheduled for this Friday, will see the French team wearing their green jersey instead of their traditional blue. ### Heatwave: Hell on board Parisian buses without air conditioning


Heatwave in Paris: public transport is under heavy strain during this extreme heat period. Buses, in particular, are often without air conditioning, making travel difficult and uncomfortable for passengers. This situation is due to a lack of investment in improving urban transport conditions. Local authorities have been criticized for their handling of this crisis, particularly for their inability to provide an effective and rapid solution for users. Temporary measures have been taken to improve the situation, such as the installation of additional air conditioning units on some buses, but these solutions are not sufficient to solve the problem in the long term. Users continue to suffer from the difficult conditions and demand better management of public transport by local authorities.

*[Source](https://www.leparisien.fr/video/actualites/paris-ile-de-france/canicule-dans-lenfer-des-bus-parisiens-sans-clim-26-06-2026-2ISOZRIDVZE73JVQR3TBHP5MJY.php)*
