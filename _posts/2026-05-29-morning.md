### End of ozone pollution alert in Northern Isère


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/s3/cruiser-production-eu3/2026/05/15ca80c7-8a49-41d3-a6a8-9b8cc8bec990/464x261_sc_sc_70777-nd3ht53vkp-whr.jpg"
    alt="End of ozone pollution alert in Northern Isère"
    caption="Fin d'alerte pollution à l'ozone en Nord-Isère" %}


End of the ozone pollution alert in the Nord-Isère department. The Prefecture has lifted the alert for level 1 pollution, which had been in effect since Tuesday, May 26, in the Lyon region and the Nord-Isère basin, due to a spike in ozone pollution. This improvement is due to a decrease in temperatures and a change in wind direction, which have allowed for better dispersion of the pollution in the atmosphere. However, authorities recommend continuing to follow preventative measures to avoid any further pollution.
