### Reims, the legendary epic: meeting with Joan of Arc


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/s3/cruiser-production-eu3/2026/05/c91fee41-f07c-44bf-baf1-e1f08a3268e7/464x261_sc_jeanne-heretick-barbe.jpg"
    alt="Reims, the legendary epic: meeting with Joan of Arc"
    caption="Reims, l'épopée légendaire : rencontre avec Jeanne d’Arc" %}


Since her childhood, Jeanne Hérétick-Barbe has been chosen to portray the legend of Joan of Arc during the Reims events, the legendary epic, which will take place on May 30th and 31st. This historical event will showcase several aspects of the life of Joan of Arc, a French heroine of the 15th century, and her crucial role in liberating Reims during the Hundred Years' War.

Preparations for these festivities are underway and include performances, exhibitions, and activities for children. Jeanne Hérétick-Barbe has already begun working on her role and has been training with a professional coach to authentically portray Joan of Arc's behavior and voice.

This event is organized by the Reims, the legendary epic association, which aims to promote the culture and history of Reims. The festivities are also supported by the city of Reims and the French Ministry of Culture.

Jeanne Hérétick-Barbe is a high school student passionate about history who has already participated in several school plays. She is very excited to have been chosen to portray Joan of Arc and hopes that these festivities will attract many visitors to Reims.

### Boxing: Tony Yoka will face Russian Murat Gassiev, WBA 'regular' champion, in July.


Boxing: Tony Yoka will face Russian Murat Gassiev, holder of the WBA "regular" title, in July. This event marks the first meeting between the two professional boxers. Gassiev, also 25 years old, won the WBA "regular" title in May 2019 after his victory over Yuniel Dorticos in a fight for the vacant title. Both boxers are known for their power and aggressiveness in their fighting style, which should result in an interesting fight.

### Political survival, fear of an agreement on the Iranian nuclear issue… Why Netanyahu is playing all his cards in Lebanon


Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu has chosen to employ his most radical strategy in the context of Lebanon, as negotiations on the Iranian nuclear agreement reach their peak.

Netanyahu stated that his country should act against Iran due to its suspicious nuclear activities and its military presence in Lebanon, particularly with Hezbollah. He also emphasized the threat that the nuclear agreement poses to Israel, as it would allow Iran to acquire the means to produce nuclear materials.

Lebanon has been grappling with a deep political crisis for several years, with a division between supporters of Hezbollah and those who seek greater independence from Iran. Netanyahu has chosen to align himself with the opponents of Hezbollah in order to prevent their consolidation of power in Lebanon, and thus limit Iranian influence in the region.

Netanyahu's actions have been criticized by some as an attempt to disrupt negotiations on the Iranian nuclear agreement, while others have emphasized that they are necessary to protect Israel from the Iranian threat.

In any case, the events unfolding in Lebanon and the negotiations on the Iranian nuclear agreement are likely to have significant consequences for regional security and for relations between Israel and its neighbors.

### Social leasing, 'big car' incentives, home assistance... At least 130,000 electric cars will be supported by the end of 2026.


Social leasing for electric vehicles is booming in France, with projections of over 130,000 electric vehicles being financed through this method by the end of 2026. ### Teenager strangled in Rennes: two 15 and 16-year-olds held in custody for 'murder'


In France, two teenagers, aged 15 and 16, have been arrested and detained for murder following the strangulation of a child in Rennes. ### 'We understand that it's festive, but it's dangerous': in Paris, anti-drunkenness operation on the Saint-Martin canal


On May 1, 2023, Parisian authorities carried out an operation aimed at banning illegal diving in the Saint-Martin Canal in Paris. This measure was taken due to the dangers associated with diving activities in this location, which is also used for supplying drinking water to the capital. Authorities reminded people that illegal diving can lead to serious accidents and endanger public health. The operation was carried out by the municipal police, the national gendarmerie, and the fire and rescue service (SIS). Authorities also called on Parisians to respect water safety rules and to avoid unnecessary risks.
