## Isère


### A future factory at €700 million in Isère to produce e-methanol without relying on the Strait of Hormuz.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/s3/cruiser-production-eu3/2026/04/7a4ad9ab-c9da-46a4-b9d2-6959b2467d5c/464x261_sc_capture.jpg"
    alt="A future factory at €700 million in Isère to produce e-methanol without relying on the Strait of Hormuz."
    caption="Une future usine à 700 millions d'euros en Isère pour produire du e-méthanol sans dépendre du détroit d'Ormuz" %}


A 700 million euros industrial project is approved for the construction of an e-methanol production plant in Isère. This plant will be established on the chemical platform of Roches-Roussillon and marks one of the largest French industrial projects. The objective is to reduce dependence on imported methanol, particularly via the Strait of Hormuz. The commission investigator has given his approval for this project.

### Winter Games 2030: due to difficulties encountered in Nice, potential relocation of men's ice hockey to Paris or Lyon.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/s3/cruiser-production-eu3/2026/04/4751b137-b776-429e-8cce-fb5adef9637f/464x261_sc_maxsportsworldthree385540.jpg"
    alt="Winter Games 2030: due to difficulties encountered in Nice, potential relocation of men's ice hockey to Paris or Lyon."
    caption="JO d'hiver 2030 : face aux difficultés rencontrées à Nice, possible déplacement du hockey masculin à Paris ou Lyon" %}


The Winter Olympic Games 2030 are preparing, and the initially chosen city to host all ice events is Nice. However, encountered difficulties have led the Organizing Committee of the Winter Olympics 2030 (Cojop) to announce on Tuesday that it was considering moving the men's ice hockey tournament to Lyon or Paris. Initially, Nice was supposed to host all ice events of the Winter Olympics 2030, but technical and logistical problems have been identified which make it difficult to set up the necessary facilities for men's ice hockey. The Cojop is currently in discussions with the cities of Lyon and Paris to determine where the tournament will be finally organized.

*[Source](https://www.ici.fr/provence-alpes-cote-d-azur/alpes-maritimes-06/nice/jo-d-hiver-2030-face-aux-difficultes-rencontrees-a-nice-possible-deplacement-du-hockey-masculin-a-paris-ou-lyon-6103359?at_medium=lien_RSS&at_locale=29)*

### Lufthansa forbids the director of 'Mr. Nobody Against Putin' from boarding with his Oscar... and loses his statuette.


The film "Mr. Nobody vs Poutine" director was prevented by Lufthansa from boarding a flight, which resulted in the loss of his Oscar. The director, Akiva Goldsman, had received the Oscar for Best Adapted Screenplay for the film "A Beautiful Mind" in 2002. He was on his way to the opening of the Moscow International Film Festival when Lufthansa refused him boarding, as he did not have the necessary documents for his Oscar. The director was forced to leave his statuette at the airport and buy a new one to be able to attend the festival. Lufthansa justified its decision by saying it does not allow passengers to carry valuable objects on its flights, but it was also criticized for treating inappropriately an Oscar winner.
