### Cycling around the Thau Lagoon


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/a42fcc2b-8c6c-433a-92da-92ee11775a94/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Cycling around the Thau Lagoon"
    caption="Le tour de l'étang de Thau à vélo" %}


The cycling route around Thau Lake offers a pleasant day trip. The route passes through the towns of Marseillan, Meze, and Bouzigues. Thau Lake is a protected natural area that is home to a variety of marine and terrestrial species. Cyclists can enjoy views of the lake throughout the route, as well as the picturesque landscapes of the villages and onion fields. The ride is ideal for nature lovers, cyclists, and foodies, as it allows you to discover local products such as Meze muscadet or Marseillan aioli. It is important to note that the route can be long (approximately 30 km) and that there are areas with car traffic, so it is recommended to be vigilant and to follow the rules of the road.
