Senator Laurent Duplomb (LR), co-rapporteur of the emergency bill for the protection and sovereignty of agriculture, visited a farm in Val-de-Virieu, Isère. The bill will be examined in the Senate on June 30th. Duplomb defended his vision of agriculture, which opposes practices such as the use of pesticides, hunting wolves, and water retention. He emphasized the importance of protecting the environment and supporting local farmers. The bill aims to strengthen regulations in the field of sustainable agriculture, particularly in relation to the protection of endangered species and the management of water.

### Drôme des collines. Why do these campsites struggle to recruit seasonal workers?

    caption="Drôme des collines. Pourquoi ces campings peinent à recruter des saisonniers ?" %}


Camping sites in the Drôme des Collines are struggling to recruit seasonal workers at the beginning of each season. This can be attributed to several reasons, including an insufficient wage offer compared to other sectors, and a lack of promotion of jobs in this field. Working conditions can also be difficult due to the hot climate and the physical labor required. Furthermore, the sometimes negative experiences of seasonal workers, particularly regarding accommodation and treatment, can deter other potential candidates. To address these problems, it is necessary to offer competitive wages, provide suitable accommodation for seasonal workers, and promote jobs in this sector.
