### Walking with Etty Hillesum between Auschwitz and Amsterdam: the new challenge for Meulan's André Weill.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/s3/cruiser-production-eu3/2026/05/f229bc49-716e-4661-8221-baac9da27989/464x261_sc_sc_visuel-effet-flou.jpg"
    alt="Walking with Etty Hillesum between Auschwitz and Amsterdam: the new challenge for Meulan's André Weill."
    caption="Marcher avec Etty Hillesum entre Auschwitz et Amsterdam : le nouveau défi du meylanais André Weill" %}


André Weill, a resident of Meylan and known in the hiking world for his actions promoting peace, launched a new challenge at the age of 78. His project is titled "Marching with Etty Hillesum", which consists of performing a walk between Auschwitz and Amsterdam. Etty Hillesum was a young Dutch Jewish woman who was murdered in that concentration camp in 1943. Before the war, she lived in Amsterdam. André Weill wishes to honor the memory of Etty Hillesum and sensitize people to the horrors of the Holocaust by performing this pedestrian march.

### Drôme. €300 million over ten years: two social lenders want to accelerate the ecological transition of housing

    src="https://cdn-s-www.ledauphine.com/images/10901FBF-31A7-4914-83EA-4E4C7E072C46/NW_listB/300-millions-d-euros-en-dix-ans-deux-bailleurs-sociaux-veulent-accelerer-la-transition-ecologique-des-logements-1778416110.jpg"
    alt="Drôme. €300 million over ten years: two social lenders want to accelerate the ecological transition of housing"
    caption="Drôme. 300 millions d’euros en dix ans : deux bailleurs sociaux veulent accélérer la transition écologique des logements" %}


The department of Drôme has announced that two social real estate organizations, Valence Romans Habitat (VRH) and Drôme Amenagement Habitat (DAH), will invest a combined total of 300 million euros over a ten-year period to accelerate the ecological transition of housing. Together, they represent approximately 20,000 homes and 40,000 tenants in the department. The two organizations play a significant role in the local rental and real estate policy of the department. Since 2022, they have been grouped together in a coordination society. The common ambitions of the main social landlords are considered crucial for the ecological transition of housing in Drôme.
