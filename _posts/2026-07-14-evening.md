### Germany recorded 32% more deaths during the June heatwave.


Germany recorded a 32% increase in deaths during the June heatwave. This increase is due to a higher number of deaths related to extreme heat, which reached record temperatures in some parts of the country. Health authorities have also reported an increase in emergency admissions for cardiovascular and respiratory diseases. It is important to note that the heatwave also caused forest fires and water shortages in some regions. Experts have emphasized the importance of taking into account the potential effects of the heatwave on public health when forecasting future weather.
