### Nord-Isère : more than 30,000 people expected at the traditional first of May fair in Bourgoin-Jallieu

The department of Nord-Isère will welcome an estimated crowd of over 30,000 people for the traditional first of May fair in Bourgoin-Jallieu. This event takes place every year on May 1st and will gather more than 300 exhibitors this year.

### Who are today's Don Quixotes?


People who can be compared to Don Quixote of today are those who, in their daily life, stand out for their idealism and devotion to values they consider noble or indisputable. These individuals tend to be optimistic, romantic, and have a utopian vision of the world. They are often criticized for their naivety and inability to conform to the realities of modern life. However, they continue to persevere in their actions inspired by the values they hold dear, such as honor, justice, or courage. They can be found in various fields, ranging from politics to art, through science and leisure.
