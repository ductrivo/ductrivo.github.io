Wildfires have ravaged approximately 1,200 hectares in the departments of Aude, Hérault, and Bouches-du-Rhône in southern France on Thursday. Authorities are currently managing several ongoing fires in southern France.

### World Cup: When are yellow cards overturned?


The World Cup is an international football competition organized by the Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA). Yellow and red cards are sanctions applied to players for infractions committed on the field. Yellow cards are not automatically nullified during a World Cup, but they can be retained until the end of the competition if a player accumulates two or three yellow cards. When a player receives three yellow cards, they are suspended for the next match. If a player receives a direct red card, they are automatically suspended for at least the next match of their team. The rules of FIFA can be modified for each World Cup, but the yellow and red cards generally remain in effect throughout the competition.
