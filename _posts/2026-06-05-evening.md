### A hike from Varces to Crolles to raise awareness about the dangers of the production of electronic components in Isère.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/s3/cruiser-production-eu3/2026/06/492d12c9-b8a2-4b3a-9683-d2a3d633cbb2/464x261_sc_whatsapp-image-2026-06-05-at-18-15-56.jpg"
    alt="A hike from Varces to Crolles to raise awareness about the dangers of the production of electronic components in Isère."
    caption="Une randonnée de Varces à Crolles pour alerter contre les dangers de la production de puces électroniques en Isère" %}


Between June 4th and 7th, 2023, members of the STopMicro collective undertook a hike from Varces to Crolles in order to raise awareness about the risks associated with the production of electronic components in Isère. The group covered approximately 50 kilometers to inform the public about the potential negative consequences of this industry on the environment and human health.

On Friday, they stopped at the scientific peninsula of Grenoble to eat together. This hike was organized as part of a campaign to highlight the polluting practices and difficult working conditions that can be found in the electronic component production industry.

The STopMicro collective also called for a review of environmental and health standards in the production of electronic components, as well as a stronger commitment from companies to protect the environment and human health.

*[Source](https://www.ici.fr/auvergne-rhone-alpes/isere-38/grenoble/une-randonnee-de-varces-a-crolles-pour-alerter-contre-les-dangers-de-la-production-de-puces-electroniques-en-isere-1195387?at_medium=lien_RSS&at_locale=29)*
