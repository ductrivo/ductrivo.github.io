### Heatwave: 26 departments placed on orange alert this Thursday


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/1b9ac478-7cbd-409b-b335-951e8757add0/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Heatwave: 26 departments placed on orange alert this Thursday"
    caption="Canicule : 26 départements placés en vigilance orange ce jeudi" %}


Heatwave: Twenty-six departments are on orange alert starting this Thursday at noon, according to the latest bulletin published by Météo France at 4 pm on Wednesday. The affected departments range from the Paris basin to the center of the country, towards Grand-Est and the north of Rhône-Alpes. This situation is due to a heatwave, which means a period of intense and prolonged heat. It is important for citizens to protect themselves from the harmful effects of the heat by taking precautions such as regular hydration, wearing light clothing, and limiting outdoor physical activity during the hottest hours.
