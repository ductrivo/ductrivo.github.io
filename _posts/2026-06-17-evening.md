### Truck drivers' refusal to comply with traffic regulations has increased by 10% in France in 2025.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/c1ed7aa8-acad-4b00-83cb-2b9e5f71783e/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Truck drivers' refusal to comply with traffic regulations has increased by 10% in France in 2025."
    caption="Les refus d'obtempérer routiers ont augmenté de 10% en France en 2025" %}


There has been a 10% increase in instances of truckers refusing to comply with traffic regulations in France in 2025, with a total of over 28,000 cases reported on French roads. According to figures from the Ministry of the Interior's Statistical Service (SSMSI), this rate represents an increase compared to the previous year, 2024.

### Cycling around the Thau Lagoon


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/a42fcc2b-8c6c-433a-92da-92ee11775a94/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Cycling around the Thau Lagoon"
    caption="Le tour de l'étang de Thau à vélo" %}


The cycling route around Thau Lake offers a pleasant day trip. The route passes through the towns of Marseillan, Meze, and Bouzigues. Thau Lake is a saltwater lake located in the Hérault department in the Occitanie region of France. This cycling trip allows you to discover the nature and landscapes of the Thau basin, as well as its villages and local products such as oysters and mussels. The route is approximately 30 kilometers long and offers views of the lake and its surroundings. Cyclists are recommended to have a map or guide to follow the route correctly.

### Between 300,000 and 1.5 million: Why the city of Jakarta is overrun with stray cats


The estimated number of stray cats in Jakarta ranges from 300,000 to 1.5 million. This proliferation of felines is due to several factors. Firstly, the city is very densely populated and provides a large amount of food for cats, which often feed on rats and other small animals. Secondly, animal welfare laws are not strictly enforced, which allows many cats to live on the streets without any control. Finally, the lack of an effective cat population control system also contributes to this proliferation. Stray cats pose several problems for the residents of Jakarta: they can be a source of noise and odor pollution, they can transmit diseases to humans and domestic animals, and they can also cause traffic accidents. Measures have been taken to reduce their numbers, including sterilization and vaccination campaigns, but their success is still limited.
