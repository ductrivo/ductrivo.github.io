### 'No injuries reported so far': A 7.2 magnitude earthquake hits northern Japan.


On April 1, 2023, a 7.2 magnitude earthquake struck northern Japan. According to Japanese authorities, no injuries have been reported so far. The epicenter is located in the Iwate Prefecture, about 350 kilometers northeast of Tokyo. The earthquake caused landslides and tsunamis in some coastal areas. Authorities have asked residents to remain vigilant due to the risk of aftershocks. Rescue teams are being deployed to assess the damage and assist those affected.

### Scotland-Brazil: Summary of Brazil's calm victory (3-0) over Scotland, featuring Neymar's return


International football match between Scotland and Brazil: Brazil's victory is marked by a score of 3-0, with Neymar's return. The match took place at the Krestovsky Stadium in St. Petersburg, Russia, on June 24, 2018, during the 2018 FIFA World Cup.

Neymar's return, who had been injured since February, was a significant event for Brazil. He played 71 minutes before being replaced by Firmino. Despite a mediocre performance, he contributed to Brazil's victory by making precise passes and creating several goal-scoring opportunities.
