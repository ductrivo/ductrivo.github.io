Heatwave: 72 departments on red alert, 13,500 schools "closed or with adaptations". Météo France has placed 72 departments on red heatwave alert as of noon on Thursday, a record. France is about to see the very gradual end of the extreme heat episode from the west starting this Thursday evening. Follow the situation with the ICI network.

In summary, a heatwave has been declared in 72 departments in France, which is a record. More than 13,500 schools have had to close or adapt their operations. ### Oil: Brent crude falls below pre-Middle East war levels at $72.44 per barrel


The price of a barrel of Brent crude oil has fallen to $72.44, below its level before the Middle East conflict. ### In the cornfields of the Limagne region, an app aims to end the struggle of recruiting seasonal workers.


In the cornfields of the Limagne region, a mobile app called Agri-Job is being developed to address the problems related to seasonal recruitment. The app allows farmers to post temporary agricultural job openings and workers to search for job opportunities in the area. Farmers can also evaluate potential candidates online, which reduces the time required to recruit seasonal workers. The app is designed to facilitate relationships between farmers and seasonal workers, while providing a secure and efficient platform. It aims to improve the agricultural productivity of the region by reducing the time lost in recruiting seasonal workers.
