### Dame of Bonifacio: More than 50 years after its discovery, science is launching a new investigation to trace its life and its appearance.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/a2f0551b-ea1b-43db-913e-98d163242799/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Dame of Bonifacio: More than 50 years after its discovery, science is launching a new investigation to trace its life and its appearance."
    caption="Dame de Bonifacio : plus de 50 ans après sa découverte, la science relance l'enquête pour retracer sa vie et son visage" %}


The Lady of Bonifacio, discovered in 1972, remains a subject of study to trace her life and appearance. Researchers, archaeologists, and educators met this Friday at the Galéa Park in Tagliu-Isulacciu (Haute-Corse) to relaunch a large-scale scientific investigation on one of the oldest known inhabitants of Corsica. With more than 50 years since her discovery, science continues to reveal its secrets about this historical figure from ancient Corsica.

*[Source](https://www.ici.fr/corse/dame-de-bonifacio-plus-de-50-ans-apres-sa-decouverte-la-science-relance-l-enquete-pour-retracer-sa-vie-et-son-visage-2318559?at_medium=lien_RSS&at_locale=29)*
