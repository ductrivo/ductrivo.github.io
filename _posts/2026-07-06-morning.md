## Isère


### Fires: evacuations, rail traffic disrupted, several major fires currently underway in southern France


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/99ab7afe-bf6c-430d-bbf2-abf92412785f/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Fires: evacuations, rail traffic disrupted, several major fires currently underway in southern France"
    caption="Incendies : évacuations, trafic ferroviaire interrompu, plusieurs feux importants en cours dans le sud de la France" %}


Wildfires continue in southern France: several major fires have been reported on Monday. The flames have ravaged more than 4,500 hectares in the Pyrénées-Orientales, resulting in the evacuation of 10,000 people. Train traffic has also been disrupted in the region. Here is a summary of the different ongoing fires:

* Pyrénées-Orientales: more than 4,500 hectares ravaged, 10,000 people evacuated.
* Aude: a fire destroyed several houses and forced the evacuation of 200 people.
* Gard: a fire was started by an explosion of a gas pipeline, it has already ravaged more than 1,500 hectares.
* Haute-Garonne: a fire forced the evacuation of several hundred people in the commune of Saint-Julien-sur-Garonne.
* Tarn: a fire destroyed a house and forced the evacuation of 50 people.
* Lozère: a fire ravaged more than 1,000 hectares in the commune of Saint-Amans-des-Cots.

Authorities have deployed rescue teams to combat the fires and assist the evacuated people. The causes of the fires are still being investigated, but it is possible that the bad weather and excessive heat are to blame.

### The strengthened educational center in Lans-en-Vercors: providing a living environment and a project for young offenders.

The Reinforced Educational Center (CER) in Lans-en-Vercors has reopened after two years of renovations. Its goal is to offer a new perspective on life to young offenders, who will stay there for three months. These young people, placed by the justice system, are invited to relearn a new way of life in this specialized center. The CER in Lans-en-Vercors offers education and training tailored to the individual needs of young offenders, with the aim of helping these young people to reintegrate into society as responsible citizens. The center has various facilities for physical and cultural activities, as well as a school to allow them to continue their studies. The professionals at the CER work in collaboration with the justice services to ensure the successful reintegration of young people after their stay in the center.

## Île-de-France


### Delphine Jubillar Case: After Cédric Jubillar's belated confessions, a review of five years of investigation


The Delphine Jubillar case is a French legal investigation that lasted five years and recently concluded with the belated confession of her husband, Cédric Jubillar.

Delphine Jubillar, a 28-year-old woman, disappeared on July 1, 2016, in Saint-Brieuc (France). Her body was never found.

The initial investigation focused on several suspects, but Cédric Jubillar was considered one of the main suspects due to his strange behavior after his wife's disappearance and certain evidence found at their home.

However, he had never been formally charged until 2021, when he confessed to killing Delphine Jubillar in a hotel in Saint-Brieuc on the day of her disappearance. According to his confession, he then threw her into the sea.

Cédric Jubillar's confession was confirmed by physical evidence found at his home and on his mobile phone. He also confessed to destroying evidence after the crime.

The investigation is still ongoing to determine the exact circumstances of Delphine Jubillar's disappearance and to prosecute Cédric Jubillar.

*[Source](https://www.leparisien.fr/faits-divers/affaire-delphine-jubillar-apres-les-aveux-tardifs-de-cedric-jubillar-retour-sur-cinq-annees-denquete-06-07-2026-AIZSCIFRNRHMXENNFHUFWMVGUE.php)*


### Following Île-de-France, the Rouen metropolitan area is eliminating bus tickets.


In the context of the widespread deployment of the Orange card for public transport in the Haute-Normandie region, the city of Rouen has officially discontinued the use of traditional bus tickets. This measure has been planned for several years and aims to simplify the pricing system and improve the user experience. Existing subscriptions will be converted to the Orange card, which will now allow users to use all forms of public transport in the region. This measure is scheduled to take effect from January 1st of next year.
