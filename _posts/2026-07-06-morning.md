### Tour de France 2026, Stage 3: Route and profile of the day's stage between Granollers and Les Angles


The 2026 Tour de France will include a third stage between Granollers and the Angles. This mountain stage is 185 kilometers long.

The route begins in Granollers, in the Catalan province of Barcelona in Spain. The riders will then take the national road N-II to the French border near Perpignan.

After a short section on French territory, they will continue towards the Pyrenees-Orientales and cross the valleys of the Tet and Tech rivers. They will pass through the towns of Prades and Saint-Jean-Pied-de-Port before arriving at their destination, the Angles.

The stage presents several difficulties, including a first-category climb 15 kilometers from the finish, followed by a second-category climb just 3 kilometers from the arrival. These ascents will be crucial in determining the winner of the day and the first riders in the general classification.

This stage is a tribute to the region of the Pyrenees-Orientales, which has already hosted several stages of the Tour de France since its creation in 1903.
