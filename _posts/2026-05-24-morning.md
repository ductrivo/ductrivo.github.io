### Strait of Hormuz, nuclear, thawing of Iranian assets… what would the agreement between Iran and the United States foresee?


The Strait of Hormuz, the Iranian nuclear issue, and the unfreezing of Iranian assets are key topics addressed in the agreement reached between Iran and the United States.

The agreement, officially known as the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA), was signed in 2015 by seven countries, including the United States and Iran. It aimed to end the economic sanctions imposed on Iran due to its controversial nuclear program.

The Strait of Hormuz is a strategic waterway located between Saudi Arabia and Iran, which allows maritime navigation between the Persian Gulf and the Indian Ocean. The agreement provides that sanctions against Iran will be lifted in exchange for concessions on its nuclear program, including limitations on uranium enrichment activities and international access to Iranian nuclear facilities.

The Iranian nuclear issue has been the subject of many controversies and tensions between the United States and Iran, particularly due to suspicions of secret military activities related to the Iranian nuclear program. The agreement aims to end these tensions by imposing strict controls on the Iranian nuclear program and allowing inspectors from the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) to access all Iranian nuclear facilities.

The unfreezing of Iranian assets is also a key topic in the agreement. Economic sanctions have resulted in the freezing of billions of dollars in Iranian assets abroad, which has caused much suffering for Iranian citizens. The agreement provides that the frozen assets will be gradually unfrozen in exchange for concessions on the Iranian nuclear program.

In conclusion, the Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA) is an international agreement signed in 2015 between Iran and seven countries, including the United States. It aims to end the economic sanctions against Iran in exchange for concessions on its controversial nuclear program, including limitations on uranium enrichment activities and international access to Iranian nuclear facilities. The agreement also provides for the unfreezing of Iranian assets.
