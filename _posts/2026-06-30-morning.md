On May 1st in Monaco, three people were injured in an explosion in the principality, including two seriously. The incident occurred in a booby-trapped package. The Water Museum offers an educational and aquatic journey for the summer of 2026. Located in Pont-en-Royans, famous for its hanging houses and nestled at the foot of the Vercors, the museum invites visitors to explore the fascinating world of water. ### Montélimar. Raphaël Rosello, the leading candidate in the 2020 municipal elections, has died.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://cdn-s-www.ledauphine.com/images/B5EE7D09-308A-4506-B316-A001B49C9F6F/NW_listB/raphael-rosello-tete-de-liste-aux-municipales-de-2020-est-decede-1782752476.jpg"
    alt="Montélimar. Raphaël Rosello, the leading candidate in the 2020 municipal elections, has died."
    caption="Montélimar. Raphaël Rosello, tête de liste aux municipales de 2020, est décédé" %}


Montélimar: Death of Raphaël Rosello, Head of the 2020 Municipal Elections List

Raphaël Rosello, a prominent figure in Montélimar, died on Sunday, June 28th. He was the head of the list for the municipal elections in his hometown in 2020.

Rosello was an engaged politician and a dynamic entrepreneur. He served as deputy mayor of Montélimar for several terms, as well as president of the Ardèche Rhône Agglomeration syndicate. Under his leadership, the city experienced remarkable economic and cultural growth.

Rosello was also an active advocate for the environment and the protection of natural areas around Montélimar. He was recognized for his efforts in ecology and sustainable development.

His political colleagues expressed their deep sadness at his death. They highlighted his passion for Montélimar, his commitment to citizens, and his leadership in the local political scene.

The 2020 municipal elections will take place without Raphaël Rosello, but his memory will endure in the city he served with such passion and dedication.
