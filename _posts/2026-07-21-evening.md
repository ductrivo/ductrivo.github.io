### The appeal hearing for Cédric Jubillar has been postponed to the first half of 2027.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/5996deee-2255-43a7-b667-f55a980e4441/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="The appeal hearing for Cédric Jubillar has been postponed to the first half of 2027."
    caption="Le procès en appel de Cédric Jubillar reporté au premier semestre 2027" %}


The appeal trial of Cédric Jubillar, accused of the murder of his wife Delphine, has been postponed to the first half of 2027. The president of the Haute-Garonne court of assizes made this decision on the preceding Tuesday. Initially scheduled to begin on September 21, 2026, the trial is now scheduled to take place in the following first half of the year.

The concert by DJ Barbara Butch was interrupted on Saturday evening at the Cabaret Frappé in Grenoble by pro-Palestinian activists and the political party LFI. ### Giant spider, minotaur, throne of snakes: photographer Alain Laboile unveils his dreamlike garden


French photographer Alain Laboile has revealed his dreamlike garden, filled with fantastical creatures and exotic motifs. This unique place is located in the southwest of France and has become a refuge for his children and friends.

The garden is described as an enchanted forest, where trees are covered in colorful fabrics, flowers grow everywhere, and fantastical animals roam freely. The images show giant spiders, minotaurs, and thrones with snakes.

Alain Laboile began creating his garden in 2003, when he moved with his family into a dilapidated house in the woods. He began decorating the exterior of the house and garden with fabrics, toys, and found objects.

The photographer said that his goal was to create an imaginary world for his children, where they could thrive and play freely. He also explained that he wanted the garden to be a response to modern society, which is often too structured and constrained.

Alain Laboile's garden has attracted international attention and has been featured in many exhibitions and publications. It has also inspired artists and designers, who have used his images to create their own works.

Alain Laboile's garden is a unique example of human art and creativity, where the boundaries between reality and imagination are blurred, and where children can thrive in an enchanted atmosphere.
