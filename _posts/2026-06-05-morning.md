## Île-de-France


### Lihanna Case: Mayor of Fleurance laments 'a real state dysfunction'


The Lyhanna case is an ongoing political controversy in France, involving the city of Fleurance and its mayor. The mayor has expressed his dismay at what he considers to be a "real state dysfunction," in reference to administrative irregularities discovered in the renovation process of a city-owned building. According to available information, a company was chosen for the renovation without a public bidding process, which has sparked criticism and accusations of favoritism. The mayor has also been implicated in another conflict of interest, as he is alleged to have profited financially from the same renovation. Local authorities have launched an investigation to verify the allegations and determine whether any laws have been violated. This case has attracted significant media attention, as it highlights controversial administrative practices and the need to respect the rules of transparency and good governance.

### DIRECT. Disappearance of Lyanna: Gérald Darmanin to meet with all prosecutors Monday at the Prosecutor's Office


French Interior Minister Gérald Darmanin has called a meeting with all the public prosecutors next Monday at the Ministry of Justice. This meeting is being held in connection with the disappearance of Lyhanna, a 20-year-old woman who was found dead in a forest near Paris on February 14th.

The investigation into Lyhanna's death is ongoing, and authorities have refused to comment on the circumstances of her disappearance. However, there have been rumors that she may have been the victim of a sexual assault before her death.

Minister Darmanin stated that the meeting is intended to discuss how public prosecutors can collaborate to combat organized crime and sexual violence. He also emphasized the importance of conducting a thorough investigation into Lyhanna's death and ending all forms of violence against women.

Lihanna's disappearance has caused a great stir in the country, with thousands of people participating in demonstrations demanding justice and better protection for women. Authorities have promised to investigate Lyhanna's death with the utmost diligence and to implement measures to prevent such assaults in the future.
