### How to protect our birds from the heat? Tips from the LPO Occitanie


Title: How to Protect Birds from the Heat According to LPO Occitanie

To protect birds from the harmful effects of heat, it is important to apply a few simple measures. The Ligue pour la Protection des Oiseaux (LPO) Occitanie in Haute-Garonne has provided useful advice for preserving birds from the heat.

To begin, it is recommended to install a bird bath in your garden. This will allow birds to cool down and rehydrate. It is important that the bird bath is cleaned regularly to prevent the growth of bacteria or algae.

In case of extreme heat, some birds may become dehydrated. If you encounter a bird in this state, it is important to rehydrate it by offering it fresh water and food rich in water. It is also recommended to place it in a shaded area to prevent it from overheating.

It is also important not to use chemicals in your garden, as they can be toxic to birds. In addition, it is recommended to leave trees and shrubs in place to provide birds with a natural shelter from the heat.

Finally, it is important not to leave waste in your garden, as it can be dangerous for birds. By following these tips, you can help protect birds from the heat and provide them with a safe and comfortable habitat.
