### Heatwave: France smashes temperature records


Heatwave in France: Record high temperatures are being broken. The cities of Lyon, Toulouse, Bordeaux, and Marseille have experienced temperatures above 40°C. Emergency measures have been taken to protect the population from the harmful effects of this heatwave, including the establishment of hydration points and the encouragement of the use of air conditioning. Authorities have also recommended that vulnerable people stay in the shade and drink plenty of fluids. Forest fires have been reported in several regions, leading to the evacuation of local populations. The heatwave is expected to last for several more days.
