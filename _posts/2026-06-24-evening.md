## Isère


### Grenoble officially commits to organ donation with the inauguration of a symbolic plaque.


On June 22, the city of Grenoble officially supported organ donation with the inauguration of a symbolic plaque, on the occasion of National Organ Donation Day. The event was announced on Place Gre'net, a local website. The plaque is located in the city center and is intended to raise awareness among citizens about the importance of organ donation in saving lives. The city of Grenoble actively encourages awareness of this issue and hopes that this gesture will encourage more people to declare their willingness to donate their organs after their death.

*[Source](https://www.placegrenet.fr/2026/06/22/grenoble-officialise-son-engagement-en-faveur-du-don-dorganes-avec-linauguration-dun-panneau-symbolique/684013)*

The city of Grenoble has inaugurated a park in the Aubière district, near Flaubert, which was previously a zone with approximately 5,000 vehicles passing through each day. ### Drôme. 'Delaying admission to nursing homes,' the priority of the territorial resource center is to maintain seniors at home.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://cdn-s-www.ledauphine.com/images/FAC15761-23BE-4A64-AEEB-570AA86AB192/NW_listB/retarder-l-entree-en-ehpad-le-maintien-a-domicile-des-seniors-priorite-du-centre-de-ressources-territorial-1782305276.jpg"
    alt="Drôme. 'Delaying admission to nursing homes,' the priority of the territorial resource center is to maintain seniors at home."
    caption="Drôme. « Retarder l'entrée en Ehpad », le maintien à domicile des seniors, priorité du Centre de ressources territorial" %}


The Ardèche-Drôme méridionale territorial resource center, with three branches in Donzère, Bourg-Saint-Andéol, and Marsanne, was inaugurated on June 23. This center prioritizes keeping elderly people at home rather than placing them in residential care facilities (EHPADs). Its goal is to support these individuals in order to facilitate their daily lives and maintain their independence. The center's mission is to delay the entry of seniors into EHPADs, which would allow them to remain in their own environment and to live with more independence and comfort.
