{% include figure.html

The court in Soissons delivered its verdict in the case of Elisa Pilarski, who died in 2019 after being fatally mauled by a dog in a forest in the Aisne region. Christophe Ellul, the victim's partner, was sentenced to four years in prison with suspended sentence for involuntary manslaughter. In addition, the court ordered that the dog, Curtis, be euthanized.
