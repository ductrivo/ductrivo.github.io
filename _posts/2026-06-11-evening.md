### Tour Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes 2026: Which roads are closed in Savoie, Haute-Savoie, and Isère from June 12 to 14?


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/522fe47e-b79c-442f-8db7-ccf81a75f155/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Tour Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes 2026: Which roads are closed in Savoie, Haute-Savoie, and Isère from June 12 to 14?"
    caption="Tour Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes 2026 : quelles sont les routes fermées en Savoie, Haute-Savoie et en Isère du 12 au 14 juin ?" %}


The Tour d'Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes 2026 will take place on June 12, 13, and 14 and will pass through the Alps, causing significant traffic disruptions in the departments of Isère, Savoie, and Haute-Savoie. The following roads will be closed:

* In the department of Isère: National Road 85 between Grenoble and Saint-Jean-de-Maurienne, as well as Departmental Road 1091 between Saint-Jean-de-Maurienne and Modane.
* In the department of Savoie: National Road 201 between Albertville and Moûtiers, National Road 90 between Aigueblanche and Bourg-Saint-Maurice, as well as Departmental Road 308 between Bourg-Saint-Maurice and Val-d'Isère.
* In the department of Haute-Savoie: National Road 201 between Cluses and Taninges, National Road 202 between Taninges and Thonon-les-Bains, as well as National Road 58a between Thonon-les-Bains and Evian-les-Bains.

Drivers are advised to stay informed about road closures in real-time and to avoid areas with disrupted traffic if possible.
