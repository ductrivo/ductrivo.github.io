## Île-de-France


### Courbevoie: A general practitioner stabbed six times by a patient in his office


Courbevoie: A general practitioner was attacked and stabbed six times by a patient in his office on February 1, 2023. The incident occurred around 3:30 PM, according to police sources. The doctor was urgently transported to the hospital and is currently in intensive care. The patient was arrested on the spot by the police and is in custody. The circumstances of the attack are still under investigation, but it appears that the patient is suffering from a psychosis. The Courbevoie hospital has stated that there is no risk to patients and staff.

### Exam 2026: The first part of the French exam paper is now available.


The 2026 Brevet revealed the first part of the French language exam topic. This announcement allows students and teachers to begin preparing the necessary materials for this exam. The first part of the topic consists of a reading passage, a writing task, and an oral question. The themes covered in the reading passage are diverse and cover topics such as the environment, social relationships, culture, and history. The writing task is also varied and allows students to demonstrate their understanding and mastery of the French language. The oral question is designed to assess students' ability to communicate orally in French. Teachers can use the provided materials to adapt their teaching to the needs and interests of their students.

*[Source](https://www.leparisien.fr/etudiant/college/brevet/brevet-2026-le-sujet-de-lepreuve-de-francais-QNTB2JFGQFH5TBONSB3QXFM4AM.php)*


### World Cup: Why has the record for the most goals scored in a World Cup already been broken during the group stage?


The World Cup in question is undoubtedly the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia. For the first time in its history, the record for the most goals scored in a single edition was broken in the group stage. Indeed, following the matches played up to June 24, the total number of goals scored already exceeds the 103 goals scored in the 2006 World Cup in Germany. This performance is due to an offensive strategy and a more offensive style of play by the participating teams, who have scored more goals than in previous editions. However, there are still several matches to be played, particularly the final phase, which could further modify the total number of goals scored.

*[Source](https://www.leparisien.fr/sports/football/coupe-du-monde/coupe-du-monde-pourquoi-le-record-du-nombre-de-buts-dans-un-mondial-a-t-il-deja-ete-battu-des-la-phase-de-poules-26-06-2026-44IYKPO6ARDBDPW2V7GZXTEZLY.php)*
