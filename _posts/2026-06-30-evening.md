### Football: Bordeaux excluded from all national competitions by the DNCG


The Girondins de Bordeaux football club has been excluded from all national competitions by the Direction Nationale du Contrôle de Gestion (DNCG) due to a significant financial deficit. This decision follows the annual evaluation of French professional clubs, which checks their ability to comply with financial and regulatory rules. Girondins de Bordeaux were found guilty of exceeding the maximum authorized debt and season ticket emission limits. This exclusion means that the club cannot participate in the next seasons of Ligue 1, Coupe de France, or Coupe de la Ligue.
