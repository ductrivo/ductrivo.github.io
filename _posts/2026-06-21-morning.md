### Heatwave: 35 departments on red alert from Sunday noon, 45 on orange alert


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/f34e41b1-169c-4307-9906-c2844dd8d443/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Heatwave: 35 departments on red alert from Sunday noon, 45 on orange alert"
    caption="Canicule : 35 départements en vigilance rouge à partir de dimanche midi, 45 en orange" %}


Heatwave: 35 departments are placed on red alert from noon on Sunday, 45 on orange. Thirty-five departments will go on red alert on Sunday at noon due to a heatwave that is affecting the country, Météo France indicates on Saturday in its 4 pm bulletin. This is the highest level of alert. 45 will be on orange. The temperature could reach 41°C "in some areas".

### Living with Charcot's disease: John Scala's battle

Living with Charcot's disease: The story of John Scala describes a rare and formidable disease for which there is no cure. This disease has turned the lives of thousands of families upside down. At the age of 48, John Scala was diagnosed in 2022. Despite this, he chose to continue living his life with courage and determination. This World Charcot's Disease Day highlights his fight to raise public awareness and inform policymakers about this rare and often unknown disease.
