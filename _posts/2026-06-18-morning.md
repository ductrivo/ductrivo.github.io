### Drôme. Swimming spots: our ideas to cool down during periods of intense heat


{% include figure.html
    src="https://cdn-s-www.ledauphine.com/images/1A40EB84-AACF-4714-8F22-393FC8D5D1F4/NW_listB/coins-de-baignade-nos-idees-pour-vous-rafraichir-en-periode-de-forte-chaleur-1781707904.jpg"
    alt="Drôme. Swimming spots: our ideas to cool down during periods of intense heat"
    caption="Drôme. Coins de baignade : nos idées pour vous rafraîchir en période de forte chaleur" %}


The Drôme offers several swimming spots to cool off during the high temperatures expected this week, with temperatures potentially reaching 40°C in mainland France. Although there are many swimming spots, both paid and free, here are some suggestions to help you find the best place to cool off:

1. Monteynard-Avignonet Lake: This artificial lake is a popular spot for swimming and water sports. It has a beach, a restaurant, and an amusement park.
2. Sainte-Croix Lake: Located in the Baronnies massif, this lake is ideal for fishing, swimming, and water sports. It also has a beach, a restaurant, and a campsite.
3. Chambon Lake: This natural lake is located in the Vercors massif. It offers opportunities for fishing, swimming, and hiking. It also has a beach and a restaurant.
4. Gorges de l'Oule: These gorges are ideal for hiking and exploring nature. They also offer opportunities for swimming in the cool, clear waters.
5. Chambaran Lake: This artificial lake is located near Valence. It has a beach, a restaurant, and water sports activities such as sailing, canoeing, and water skiing.
6. Saint-Pierre Lake: This natural lake is located in the Diois mountains. It offers opportunities for fishing, swimming, and hiking. It also has a beach and a restaurant.
