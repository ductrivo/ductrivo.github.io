### Water management in the garden: watering techniques


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/a68a2005-b643-4eda-a62e-4c873869e3c6/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Water management in the garden: watering techniques"
    caption="La gestion de l'eau au jardin : les techniques d'arrosage" %}


Water management in the garden: irrigation techniques. What is the best irrigation technique? Manual, localized, or sprinkler? Here are tips from gardener and landscaper Bastien Glomot to help you in your garden.

Water management is crucial for the health and proper development of plants. There are several irrigation techniques, each with its advantages and disadvantages.

Manual watering involves using a watering can or tap to water plants. This allows you to control the amount of water given to the plants, but it can be time-consuming and require a lot of time and effort.

Localized watering involves using irrigation systems such as micro-drip or drip emitters to water plants directly at the root level. This allows for more efficient watering and better water control, but it can be expensive to install.

Sprinkler watering involves using irrigation systems such as watering cans or sprinklers to water plants using a large amount of water. This can be effective for irrigating large areas, but it can also lead to water loss and water waste.

It is important to choose the watering technique that is best suited for your garden, depending on the size of your garden, the type of soil, the climate, and the plants you have. It is also important to avoid wasting water and to regularly regulate watering to prevent overwatering or water shortages.

Finally, it is important to remember that water management in the garden is not just about watering, but also about collecting and reusing water. It is possible to collect rainwater to use in your garden, which can be an effective way to reduce irrigation costs and conserve water resources.

*[Source](https://www.ici.fr/emissions/au-jardin-ici-creuse/la-gestion-de-l-eau-au-jardin-les-techniques-d-arrosage-6427452?at_medium=lien_RSS&at_locale=29)*
