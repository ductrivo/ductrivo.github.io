### Uruguay - Spain (0-1): Summary of Spain's victory, which eliminates Uruguay and qualifies Cape Verde


The match between Uruguay and Spain ended in a 0-1 victory for Spain, eliminating Uruguay from the competition. This victory allows Spain to qualify for the quarter-finals, and Cape Verde to also qualify as the best second in their group.

Spain dominated the match, with superior ball possession and numerous scoring opportunities. The Spanish goal was scored by Ferran Torres in the 27th minute, after a precise cross from Jordi Alba. Uruguay failed to create many offensive opportunities and often struggled to contain the Spanish attack.

Cape Verde finished second in their group due to their 1-0 victory over Portugal, which took place simultaneously. The two teams ended with the same number of points, but Cape Verde had a better goal difference and was therefore qualified for the quarter-finals.

This result marks the first time that Uruguay has been eliminated in the first round of a World Cup since 1950, when they were the defending champions. Spain, on the other hand, continues its progress in the World Cup and hopes to maintain its good form for the quarter-finals.
