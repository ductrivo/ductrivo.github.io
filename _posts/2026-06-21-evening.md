### 206,054 counterfeit branded products seized: the counterfeit goods court in Marseille


The Anti-Counterfeiting Unit, located in Marseille, seized 206,054 counterfeit goods infringing on intellectual property rights. This operation was carried out by the relevant authorities to protect the rights of legitimate brands and preserve the integrity of the market. The seized goods included clothing, accessories, shoes, and other common consumer items, all bearing fraudulently marked trademarks. The operation was carried out in collaboration with police and customs services to ensure an effective response to counterfeiting. The seized goods will be examined to determine their market value and final destination, and then they will be destroyed or returned to the legitimate owners of the trademarks. This operation is an important measure to protect consumers from substandard goods and to protect the French industry from counterfeiting.
