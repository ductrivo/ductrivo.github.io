### Bigorre outing deals (July 13-19, 2026)

2. Pyrenees Animal Park: Travel to the animal world with over 100 species of mammals, birds and reptiles in their natural habitat.
3. Arboucave Cave: Explore the largest decorated cave in the department, with remarkable stalactites and stalagmites.
4. Bear Bernard Park: Discover brown bears in their natural habitat, as well as other local animal and plant species.

6. Regional Natural Park of the Pyrenees Ariégeoises: Enjoy a day in nature with hiking trails, spectacular views and rich fauna and flora.
7. Louron Lake: Discover this mountain lake ideal for fishing, boating or simply enjoying a moment of relaxation in nature.
8. Les Aigles de Bigorre Adventure Park: Try activities such as the aerial course, bungee jumping and segway for a day full of adrenaline.
9. Vieux-Pau Museum: Discover the history of the city of Pau with exhibitions on its royal past, traditions and architectural heritage.
10. Nay Wind Farm: Enjoy a guided tour to learn more about wind turbines and their impact on the environment.
