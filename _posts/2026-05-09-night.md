The French Canon is a controversial group that promotes a nationalist and identity-based vision of France, leading to accusations of racism and extremism. Critics claimed that the event was a manifestation of these ideas.

The banquet took place at a hotel in Montélimar and included speeches, concerts, and sports activities. Some participants wore military clothing or controversial symbols, which sparked negative reactions on social media.

Organizers claimed that the event was a gathering for French people of French origin and not intended to promote controversial ideas. However, some observers pointed out that the French Canon has been linked to violent incidents in the past, strengthening the controversy surrounding the event.

The French Canon banquet sparked strong reactions from the media and French political groups. Some called for its dissolution, while others defended the right of French citizens to gather freely.

The debate around the French Canon and its festive event continues currently in France, with divided opinions on how it should be handled by authorities and French society.
