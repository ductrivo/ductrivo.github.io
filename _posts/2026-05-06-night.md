### A simple 'hello' may be enough: what is the scam with silent calls, on the rise with artificial intelligence?


The silent call scam is a fraudulent practice that involves contacting people by phone without giving them time to answer or block the calls. Fraudsters often use automated technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI) to generate fictitious phone numbers and simulate calls from legitimate companies.

Victims receive a silent call, which does not allow time to respond or block the call. Fraudsters can then use the victims' phone numbers to commit other crimes, such as sending text messages or phishing emails, or selling their information to other criminals.

This practice is illegal in many countries and can lead to legal action. Authorities recommend that people never answer a silent call, block unknown numbers, and report suspicious calls to the police.

The use of artificial intelligence by fraudsters to commit crimes such as the silent call scam is a growing problem because it allows criminals to reach more people in less time. Authorities and companies must therefore take measures to prevent these fraudulent practices and protect their clients against cyber threats.
