### Low risk for the WHO as Hantavirus is not very contagious on a cruise ship.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/s3/cruiser-production-eu3/2026/05/9744c262-c653-4a01-a5ea-e33297bd78a5/140x140_sc_000-a9yr2uh.jpg"
    alt="Low risk for the WHO as Hantavirus is not very contagious on a cruise ship."
    caption="Hantavirus sur un navire de croisière : le risque est 'faible' pour l'OMS car le virus 'n'est pas très contagieux'" %}


On May 6, the World Health Organization (WHO) evaluated the risk of a hantavirus outbreak on a cruise ship as low. Three deaths were recorded on the ship in the Atlantic, causing concern. However, the virus is not very contagious from person to person, significantly reducing the risk of widespread transmission. A scientist emphasized that the virus is primarily transmitted by humans coming into contact with urine and feces of sick rats. Preventive measures such as avoiding contact with these animals, regularly cleaning public spaces, and using masks are recommended to reduce infection risk. The WHO also called on passengers and crew of the ship to follow the ship's leadership instructions and adhere to preventive measures.

The three young men from Isère have been condemned by the Grenoble Judicial Court for organizing an aggravated prostitution network involving minors from the Grenoble agglomeration. ### Ted Turner, founder of the American news channel CNN, died at the age of 87.

### Sexual assault suspicions in a renowned dance and musical comedy school in Nice.


In the city of Nice, suspicions of sexual assaults have been raised against a renowned dance and musical comedy school. According to available information, several students have accused teachers of inappropriate behavior. Local authorities have launched an investigation to look into these allegations.

The school in question is known for producing many talented artists in the fields of dance and musical comedy. However, these accusations have brought potential issues of inappropriate behavior in the dance and musical comedy industry to light.

Students have declared they have faced sexual advances, intimidation demands, and threats when refusing to cooperate with certain teachers. Authorities have also received complaints about the school's inappropriate management by its leaders.

The allegations have been widely spread on social media, causing a strong reaction from the local and national artistic community. Students have also created a support group to share their experiences and help put an end to inappropriate practices in the industry.

Local authorities have called for greater transparency and measures to protect students against sexual assault. Investigations are still ongoing, but the allegations have already led to changes in the school's management and strengthened security measures.

This case highlights the importance of a safer and more respectful culture in art schools, as well as throughout the entire artistic industry. Local authorities have called for collaboration between students, teachers, and leaders to create a safer environment for all artists.
