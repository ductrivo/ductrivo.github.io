### Will Corsica have a 'Parliament of the Sea' before the end of the year?


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/a9b99dc3-7dc0-441c-9e9a-4ba182326956/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Will Corsica have a 'Parliament of the Sea' before the end of the year?"
    caption="La Corse dotée d'un 'Parlement de la mer' avant la fin de l'année ?" %}


Corsica could have a Sea Parliament before the end of 2023. This project was presented in Ajaccio by the French Institute for the Mediterranean Sea in Corsica. Its aim is to bring together elected officials, economic actors, associations and experts to better coordinate island maritime policies. The Sea Parliament would be intended to bring together local decision-makers to discuss issues related to the sea and fishing, as well as to identify sustainable solutions to protect the Corsican marine environment.

*[Source](https://www.ici.fr/corse/la-corse-dotee-d-un-parlement-de-la-mer-avant-la-fin-de-l-annee-7986883?at_medium=lien_RSS&at_locale=29)*

### VIDEO - In this educational farm, the animals participate in teaching how to live together.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/606a6924-7783-407d-96b7-5afdc6ddbbc5/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="VIDEO - In this educational farm, the animals participate in teaching how to live together."
    caption="VIDÉO - Dans cette ferme pédagogique, les animaux participent à enseigner le vivre-ensemble" %}


This text describes an educational farm located in Jonchery-sur-Vesle, in the Marne department. The establishment was founded four years ago by two friends, Marie and Sophie, who aim to share their passion for animals with as many people as possible. The educational farm is designed to teach coexistence through interaction with animals. Visitors can learn to understand and respect animals, while also enjoying an educational and fun experience. The farm houses various animal species, such as dogs, cats, rabbits, chickens, and geese. The owners also emphasize the quality of life for the animals by providing them with a healthy and pleasant environment. The educational farm is open to the public and offers guided tours for schools, as well as specialized activities such as ecological education workshops.

### 'David Hockney was a humble and very private man': Beuvron-en-Auge, the Norman land of inspiration for the deceased British painter


David Hockney was a deceased British artist from Yorkshire, who was strongly inspired by Normandy, and particularly by Beuvron-en-Auge. He is known for his humility and discretion. His work spanned several decades and was characterized by a great diversity of styles, ranging from realism to abstraction. His paintings have been exhibited in many prestigious galleries and are now considered masterpieces of modern art. He also worked in other fields such as photography, design, and film. His death in 2016 was a significant event for the art world.

### 'Recurring threatening behavior': Sweden intercepts two Russian hunters near its airspace


Swedish authorities intercepted two Russian fighter jets on July 1, violating Swedish airspace. According to reports, the aircraft were spotted near the Swedish exclusive economic zone in the Baltic Sea. Swedish authorities stated that this was a recurring and threatening behavior and demanded an explanation from Russian authorities. This is the third intrusion into Swedish airspace in two weeks, raising concerns about regional security. The Swedish authorities also stated that they had alerted the Norwegian and Finnish military authorities to be prepared to respond if necessary.
