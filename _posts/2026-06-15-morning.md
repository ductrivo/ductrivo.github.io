### Tauromachy, a bull from Pagès Mailhan, spared during the bullfight in Mimizan


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/005f6458-8b3e-4624-8aeb-c45ae45d02fd/920x517?webp=false"

Bullfighting was an event that took place in Mimizan, France, last Sunday. The bulls came from Pagès Mailhan and were intended for Jesus Enrique Colombo, Solal, and Ismaël Martin. The fifth skilled bull, born in April 2022, was spared, which is a first in the history of the Courant bullring in Mimizan. ### Win your family pass for the Marquèze Eco-Museum in Sabres by identifying a typical object from the moorland.

### Paris Basketball-Monaco (95-91): The capital club launches its title defense in the Betclic Élite final perfectly.
