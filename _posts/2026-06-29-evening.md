### What does the reform of professional sports and football governance voted by the deputies contain?


The reform of professional sports and football governance, voted by parliamentarians, aims to improve transparency, regulation, and fairness in the world of football. It includes several important measures:

1. Creation of a new public entity, the High Authority of the Professional Football League (LFP), responsible for managing professional French competitions and ensuring the application of the rules.
2. Limiting the number of consecutive terms for the presidents of professional football clubs, in order to prevent the concentration of power.
3. Obligation for clubs to publish their annual accounts and justify their financial management.
4. Creation of a solidarity fund to help clubs in financial difficulties.
5. Prohibition of transfers between clubs, in order to prevent the creation of joint debts.
6. Obligation for clubs to implement a sustainable development and equal opportunities plan.
7. Creation of an independent body responsible for combating doping in French football.
8. Obligation for clubs to provide ongoing training for referees.
9. Prohibition of transfers of minors, in order to protect young footballers.
10. Creation of an independent body responsible for regulating player transfers and ensuring their protection.

This reform aims to improve the governance of French football, to protect young footballers, and to guarantee greater financial transparency in the world of professional football.

### Kylian Mbappé, the weight of words


Title: Kylian Mbappé, the importance of words

Kylian Mbappé is a French football player born in 1998 in Bondy. He currently plays for Paris Saint-Germain and the French national team.

Mbappé began his professional career at AS Monaco, where he quickly established himself as one of the best young talents in French football. In 2017, he joined Paris Saint-Germain for a record transfer fee of 180 million euros.

With PSG, Mbappé has won several national and European titles, including the Champions League in 2020. He is considered one of the best players in the world and was named Ballon d'Or in 2019.

Outside of his sporting career, Mbappé is known for his social and political activism. He has been involved in several campaigns to combat racism and homophobia in football, as well as to promote education and children's rights.

In 2021, Mbappé was involved in a controversy after criticizing the living conditions of Paris Saint-Germain players in an interview with the French magazine "L'Équipe". He was also criticized for his statements about French politics and his support for Emmanuel Macron during the 2022 presidential election.

Mbappé is considered one of the most talented players of his generation, but also one of the most controversial due to his public statements and political engagement. He continues to be one of the most popular players in the world of football.
