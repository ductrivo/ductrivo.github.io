### Forest fires: Vaucluse on red alert for 'very high danger level', seven departments in the south on orange alert


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/84b613c9-9130-4c80-85a7-ac099bf6eb93/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Forest fires: Vaucluse on red alert for 'very high danger level', seven departments in the south on orange alert"
    caption="Feux de forêts : le Vaucluse en vigilance rouge pour 'niveau de danger très élevé', sept départements du Sud en orange" %}


Wildfires have put the Vaucluse on high alert for a "very high danger level," while seven departments in the south are on orange alert: Pyrénées-Orientales, Aude, Hérault, Gard, Bouches-du-Rhône, Drôme, and Var. According to Météo France, the Vaucluse is on a very high danger level for wildfires this Monday. Authorities have implemented emergency measures to counter the spread of fires in these regions.

### Shopkeepers block trams to protest the progress of construction on Berriat Street in Grenoble.

    caption="Des commerçants bloquent les tramways pour dénoncer l'avancement des travaux du cours Berriat à Grenoble" %}


Shopkeepers on Berriat Street in Grenoble blocked the trams last Monday, between 8:30 and 9:00, at the Saint-Bruno stop, to protest against the progress of work on the Chronovélo line. This new line will connect Fontaine to Grenoble. The shopkeepers are concerned about the potential disruptions that the work could cause to their business. They have requested a meeting with the authorities to discuss how to manage the work and minimize the negative impact on their businesses.
