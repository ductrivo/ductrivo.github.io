### Pancreatic cancer: an innovative treatment tested on a patient in Isère, a first in Europe


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/s3/cruiser-production-eu3/2026/05/dfd74429-5f6e-4492-8bdb-d5351b6c3d25/464x261_sc_maxnewsspecialtwo581611.jpg"
    alt="Pancreatic cancer: an innovative treatment tested on a patient in Isère, a first in Europe"
    caption="Cancer du pancréas : un traitement innovant testé sur un patient en Isère, une première en Europe" %}


The pancreatic cancer was treated for the first time in Europe at the University Hospital of Grenoble (Isère) on April 22nd last, thanks to an innovative surgical intervention. During this procedure, radioactive rods were implanted directly into the pancreatic tumor. This technique is novel as it allows for the destruction of cancerous cells using a source of radioactive radiation. It represents an important step in the development of treatments for pancreatic cancer, which remains a difficult cancer to treat and cure.

### The Roux Tanneries, flagship of the leather sector in France, find themselves in turmoil


{% include figure.html
    src="https://france3-regions.franceinfo.fr/image/PGe_IufCJuUtjVMR3Ir7m5vqosQ/930x620/filters:format(webp)/regions/2026/04/29/69f2055da3ad6944103142.jpg"
    alt="The Roux Tanneries, flagship of the leather sector in France, find themselves in turmoil"
    caption="Les Tanneries Roux, fleuron du secteur du cuir en France, se retrouvent dans la tourmente" %}


The Roux Tanneries, symbol of the French leather industry, are currently facing difficulties. In 2012, the powerful luxury group LVMH acquired the Roux Tanneries, presented as a pillar of French expertise. Less than fifteen years later, the last symbol of the Romans-sur-Isère industry is threatened. The tanneries have been affected by an economic and environmental crisis, with high costs to comply with environmental standards and difficulties in finding clients willing to pay the high prices of their products. Additionally, international competition is strong in the leather sector, making it difficult for French companies to survive. The Roux Tanneries had to close a factory in 2019 and are currently in negotiations with unions to reduce the number of employees. The French government has also been approached for financial support for the company.

*[Source](https://france3-regions.franceinfo.fr/auvergne-rhone-alpes/drome/les-tanneries-roux-fleuron-du-secteur-du-cuir-en-france-se-retrouvent-dans-la-tourmente-3342803.html)*

The French Canon event organized in Montélimar was subjected to protest by the committee of struggles, who addressed the mayor to express their concerns. The controversy surrounding this event does not seem to be subsiding.

The French Canon is an annual event that brings together professionals from the written press and publishing, as well as local political representatives. This edition was marked by a controversy due to the presence of controversial guests, notably a journalist known for his controversial positions on social and identity issues.

The committee of struggles denounced this presence as incompatible with the values of the city and demanded that the mayor take measures to prevent such guests from being invited in the future. The mayor responded by affirming respect for freedom of expression but stated that the city does not support the positions taken by some of the guests.

The controversy surrounding the French Canon event has attracted significant media attention and has been the subject of passionate debates on social networks. Opponents to the presence of controversial guests organized a protest against the event, while supporters have claimed their support for freedom of expression and the right to gather to discuss important issues.

The mayor confirmed that he would not take a position on the positions taken by the banquet's guests, but that Montélimar would remain open to debates and exchanges of ideas. He also affirmed his commitment to promoting values of tolerance and respect in Montélimar.
