The association "Helpers and Helped in Auvergne" was founded in 2024 with a mission to help caregivers in the Auvergne region. Its main objective is to allow caregivers to relax and reduce their stress, which are often high due to the intense work they perform.

This association offers specific services for young caregivers, who can sometimes be minors. It strives to provide emotional and practical support to caregivers so they can continue working with confidence and effectiveness.

The services provided by the association include continuing education workshops, discussion groups, recreational activities, and meetings between caregivers to share their experiences and skills. The association also works in collaboration with local public services to offer additional support to caregivers who need help.

The association "Helpers and Helped in Auvergne" is an important resource for caregivers in the Auvergne region, who can rely on it for emotional and practical support when they need help.

### Yvelines : three years of prison for transporting 1,180 tonnes of counterfeit cigarettes.


In the Yvelines department, a person was sentenced to three years in prison for illegally transporting 1,180 tonnes of counterfeit cigarettes. Authorities intercepted the cargo during a road control operation. The individual was found guilty of tax fraud and illegal tobacco trafficking. This case highlights the importance of fighting against counterfeit cigarette trafficking, which represents a significant loss for public finances.

### Fuel prices: there will be 'no massive cancellations' in aviation this summer, estimates the government.


The prices of fuels will not experience massive suspensions in aviation this year, according to government estimates. This decision is explained by the increase in production and transport costs of fuels, as well as an increasing demand due to the global economic recovery. However, consumers can still benefit from certain financial aids such as tax reductions or government subsidies to compensate for these price increases. Moreover, some countries have announced specific measures to limit fuel costs in the aviation sector, such as taxing large airport companies or increasing taxes on large aircraft. Finally, some experts predict that fuel prices may still increase in the coming years due to global economic growth and increasing demand for fossil fuels.

### Kvaratskhelia Ballon d'or?


The Ballon d'or is an annual award given to the best male soccer player of the year, created in 1956 by the French newspaper France Football. As of 2021, the winner of the Ballon d'or is still unknown as the votes from journalists have been postponed due to the Covid-19 pandemic. However, there are several players who are considered favorites for winning this prestigious award.

One of the most discussed candidates for the Ballon d'or 2021 is Georgian player Enzo Kvaratskhelia, who currently plays for FC Barcelona and was named in the Champions League team of the year in 2020-2021. Kvaratskhelia is considered a very talented player with excellent technique and exceptional game vision. He has also scored several important goals for his team, earning him consideration as one of the best attackers of the season.

In addition to Kvaratskhelia, there are other players who are in competition for the Ballon d'or 2021. Portuguese player Cristiano Ronaldo, who currently plays for Manchester United, is one of the favorites to win this award for the fifth time. French player Kylian Mbappé, who plays for Paris Saint-Germain, is also considered a serious candidate for the Ballon d'or 2021.

Finally, there are players such as Lionel Messi and Robert Lewandowski who have also been nominated in the list of candidates for the Ballon d'or 2021. Messi currently plays for Paris Saint-Germain after leaving FC Barcelona, while Lewandowski plays for Bayern Munich.

The winner of the Ballon d'or 2021 will be announced in November 2021 during a ceremony organized by France Football. Journalists were invited to vote for their choice of player of the year before the end of the European football season, but the votes have been postponed due to the Covid-19 pandemic.
