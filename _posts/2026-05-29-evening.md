### 'Spending time with Giono: a theatrical journey to celebrate the cabins of the Vercors'


The event "S'encabaner avec Giono: a theatrical journey to celebrate the cabins of the Vercors" is organized by the Regional Natural Park of the Vercors and the Scène nationale 7 company. This initiative transforms the area into a venue for performances, where theatrical plays inspired by the works of Jean Giono, an author associated with the Vercors, will be performed in the traditional cabins of the park. The event was announced on Place Grenet, a local website.

### Villeroy de Galhau and Michel-Édouard Leclerc at Hanouna's show: the governor of the Bank of France is still a victim of a fake


The Governor of the Bank of France, Villeroy de Galhau, has been the victim of a false rumor spread on social media. The rumor claimed that he had resigned from his position due to disagreements with the French government. However, the Bank of France quickly denied this information and confirmed that Villeroy de Galhau remains in his position.

During a television interview on Hanouna, he was questioned by Michel-Édouard Leclerc on this topic. Villeroy de Galhau explained that there was no truth to these rumors and that his term at the Bank of France is still ongoing. He also emphasized the importance of institutional stability in maintaining the confidence of the financial markets.

This case illustrates the dangers of false information circulating on social media and its potential impact on financial markets. It is important to verify sources before sharing information, in order to avoid causing panic or damaging the trust in financial institutions.

### 'The Enfoires aim to be impeccable': why Patrick Bruel is withdrawing from the Restos du Coeur troupe


The group Les Enfoirés, known for its annual charity performances for Restos du Cœur, has announced that singer Patrick Bruel will not participate in the 2023 edition. According to an official statement, Bruel has decided to take a break to focus on other projects. It is specified that he will remain a member of the group and that his participation may be resumed in the following years. Patrick Bruel was one of the founding members of Les Enfoirés in 1990 and has participated in all performances since then, except in 2004 and 2018.
