Employees of the Institute of Biology and Pathology of the University Hospital (CHU) of Grenoble have reported symptoms such as nauseating odors, headaches, and general malaise for the past seven years. Valencia is presenting a project for a high-level service bus line, which will be faster, along the current route of line C1. ### A Lufthansa Boeing's nose suddenly drops at Frankfurt airport, employees injured.


On March 29, 2023, an accident occurred at Frankfurt International Airport when the nose of a Lufthansa Boeing aircraft suddenly collapsed, causing injuries among the employees present.
