### Marine Le Pen: The French Supreme Court will 'do everything' to issue its decision before the presidential election.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/5fe9be8b-a6ec-4c5b-9157-3f78dd1501d5/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Marine Le Pen: The French Supreme Court will 'do everything' to issue its decision before the presidential election."
    caption="Marine Le Pen : la Cour de cassation fera 'tout' pour rendre sa décision avant la présidentielle" %}


The Court of Cassation is trying to expedite its decision on the case involving Marine Le Pen, the candidate in the upcoming French presidential election on April 18th. Remy Heitz, the chief prosecutor of the Court of Cassation, said on Thursday that the court will do everything possible to issue its decision before the first round of the election. However, he emphasized that the proceedings also depend on the parties involved in the case. Marine Le Pen is being prosecuted for having shared violent images on Twitter in 2015, which were deemed hateful and offensive. If the Court of Cassation confirms the initial conviction, Marine Le Pen could be banned from participating in the presidential election.

The heatwave is returning to Northern Isère, causing significant damage to local agriculture. ### Family outing ideas. Cycling along the Sorgue.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://cdn-s-www.ledauphine.com/images/7566D794-E4E6-4F17-82B5-8ABBD0BB2290/NW_listB/au-fil-de-la-sorgue-a-velo-1781180275.jpg"

Family Bike Rides Around Sorgue:

This 40-kilometer cycling route takes you from L'Isle-sur-la-Sorgue to Fontaine-de-Vaucluse, in the Vaucluse. This route offers a magnificent view of the Provençal landscape, with its canals, windmills, and orchards.

The starting point is in L'Isle-sur-la-Sorgue, a town known for its antique shops and markets. You then pass through the village of Menerbes, which offers a panoramic view of the Sorgue valley.

Next, you reach the village of Gordes, one of the most beautiful villages in France. You can visit its Roman church and castle.

The route continues to Fontaine-de-Vaucluse, where the Sorgue River originates. This is a popular destination for hikers and nature lovers.

This family bike ride will allow you to discover the treasures of Sorgue, while enjoying a picturesque Provençal landscape.
