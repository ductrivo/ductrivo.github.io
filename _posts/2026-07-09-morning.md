### 'You will never be able to talk about your achievements': the hidden life of the Ukrainian drone operators who attacked Moscow


Title: The Secret Lives of Ukrainian Drone Operators Attacking Moscow

This text describes the clandestine activities of Ukrainian drone operators involved in attacks against Moscow. According to the article, these drone operators were trained by Ukrainian intelligence services and have carried out several aerial raids on the Russian capital since the beginning of the Ukrainian invasion in 2014.

The drone operators use lightweight and maneuverable drones to target military and civilian targets, including energy infrastructure and government buildings. They have also used drones equipped with cameras to collect information on the positions of Russian forces.

The attacks have caused material damage and have caused some concern among the Moscow population. The Russian authorities have responded by increasing their aerial surveillance and deploying anti-drone defense systems.

Ukrainian drone operators have also been accused of attacks against civilian targets, including a hospital and a school. The Ukrainian authorities have denied these allegations and have stated that their forces do not target civilians.

This text highlights the importance of clandestine warfare in the conflict between Ukraine and Russia, as well as the growth of Ukrainian military capabilities thanks to the support of Western intelligence services. It also highlights the difficulties faced by the Russian authorities in countering these clandestine aerial attacks.
