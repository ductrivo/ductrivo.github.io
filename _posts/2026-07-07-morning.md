### The Green Party's European Parliament member, Rima Hassan, is on trial for inciting terrorism.


The French MP, Rima Hassan, of La France Insoumise (Unbowed France), is currently on trial for accusations of inciting terrorism. The charges are based on comments she made on Facebook in 2015, in which she compared Israeli attacks on Gaza to war crimes and crimes against humanity. The trial began on May 3, 2023, at the Paris criminal court. Hassan's defense argues that her comments were expressions of opinion and could not be interpreted as an incitement to terrorism. The expected verdict could have significant implications for freedom of expression in France, particularly in the context of combating discrimination and stigmatization.
