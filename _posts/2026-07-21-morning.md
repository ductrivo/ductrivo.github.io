### Agricultural Emergency Law: Despite the return of acetamipride, deputies adopt the text in a tense atmosphere.


The Emergency Agricultural Law has been adopted by the deputies, despite the controversy surrounding the reintroduction of acetamipride. This law has been approved in a tense atmosphere. It aims to strengthen the control of pesticides and to improve food security in France. It specifically provides for a temporary ban on the use of certain pesticides, including acetamipride, which has been reintroduced into the text after negotiations between the deputies and the government. This law is considered an important step towards more sustainable and environmentally friendly agriculture. It will be subject to review by the Senate before being enacted into law.

*[Source](https://www.leparisien.fr/politique/loi-durgence-agricole-malgre-le-retour-de-lacetamipride-les-deputes-adoptent-le-texte-dans-un-climat-electrique-21-07-2026-F6Y6ALAHUZDYXMK4JDJTYKF2A4.php)*


### Nantes: a 'slum' devastated by fire, 10 hectares of vegetation destroyed


A fire has broken out in a shantytown area of Nantes, causing extensive damage. Approximately ten hectares of vegetation have been destroyed, and several buildings have been damaged or destroyed. Local authorities have declared a state of emergency to manage the situation. Hundreds of people have been evacuated from their homes, and aid is being provided to help them find temporary shelter. The cause of the fire has not yet been identified, but investigations are underway. Authorities are appealing for assistance to help extinguish the flames and limit the damage.

*[Source](https://www.leparisien.fr/faits-divers/nantes-un-bidonville-ravage-par-les-flammes-10-ha-de-vegetation-detruits-20-07-2026-INCK5Z33HNF7FCWN4KDYDXTNSU.php)*


### Near Rouen, a forest will be designated a protected area and made inaccessible to the public for 30 years.


In the Rouen region, a forest will be designated as a sanctuary for the next thirty years, meaning it will be closed to public access. This measure is being taken to allow for the protection and restoration of the forest ecosystem, as well as to promote biodiversity. The specific forest has not yet been identified, but local authorities have indicated that they are seeking to protect an area that is particularly rich in species and natural habitats. This measure is part of a broader project aimed at protecting the environment and promoting the conservation of nature in the Rouen region.
