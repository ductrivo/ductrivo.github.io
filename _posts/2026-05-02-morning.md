### 'Just an illusion' : what our nostalgia for the 1980s hides


"Juste une illusion" is what our nostalgia for the 1980s hides. This decade is often romanticized, but in reality, it was marked by numerous social and economic challenges. The 1980s saw an increase in unemployment, rising social inequalities, as well as the savings and loan crisis (S&L crisis) in the United States. In Europe, this decade was marked by the beginning of German reunification and the fall of the Berlin Wall. Despite these challenges, the 1980s also saw significant technological advancements, such as the invention of the Internet, as well as a boom in popular culture, particularly in music and cinema. However, this romanticized nostalgia for the 1980s can be considered an illusion because it fails to account for the real challenges that this period faced.

### SOS Racism alerts the defender of rights after suspicions of discrimination against black airport employees at Roissy.


The SOS Racism movement has reported suspicions of racial discrimination towards black employees at Roissy Airport in Paris, to the attention of the defender of rights. SOS Racisme has requested a thorough investigation to establish the truth and put an end to any form of discrimination.
