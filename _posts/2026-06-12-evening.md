### High temperatures: EDF warns of possible production declines in its nuclear power plant


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/b108fae6-d0b2-47cb-b6b5-cf70b3926ddc/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="High temperatures: EDF warns of possible production declines in its nuclear power plant"
    caption="Fortes chaleurs : EDF prévient de possibles baisses de production dans son parc nucléaire" %}


French energy authorities have issued warnings about a possible reduction in production at EDF's nuclear power plant, due to the current high temperatures. The electricity supplier could reduce its production, including that of the Saint-Alban nuclear power plant, located in the Isère department, starting next Tuesday. This measure would be due to an expected increase in the temperature of the Rhône River, linked to the current extreme heatwave. This is a precautionary measure to protect nuclear facilities from the risks associated with high temperatures.
