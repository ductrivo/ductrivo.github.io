### Lihanna Case: What does the 'comprehensive law' proposal against sexist and sexual violence contain?


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/46b6e31c-1518-477e-b936-012bf9a57ad3/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Lihanna Case: What does the 'comprehensive law' proposal against sexist and sexual violence contain?"
    caption="Affaire Lyhanna : ce que contient la proposition de 'loi intégrale' contre les violences sexistes et sexuelles" %}


The Lyhanna case is a controversial issue currently being debated in the country, which has prompted the President of the National Assembly to call on the government to expedite the proposed law aimed at combating sexual and gender-based violence against women and children. This proposed law, known as the "Comprehensive Law," aims to strengthen existing legal measures related to sexual and gender-based violence.

The proposed law includes several important provisions:

1. The creation of a national registry of perpetrators of sexual violence, which would allow for the tracking of cases of sexual and gender-based violence and the implementation of appropriate measures to protect victims.
2. The strengthening of protection measures for victims of sexual and gender-based violence, particularly in terms of confidentiality and witness protection.
3. The establishment of a simplified procedure for victims of sexual and gender-based violence, in order to facilitate their participation in the justice system.
4. The strengthening of measures against perpetrators of sexual violence, particularly in terms of penalties and protection measures for victims.
5. The creation of a national service for the prevention of sexual and gender-based violence, responsible for raising public awareness of these issues and providing support services to victims.

The Lyhanna case has generated a great deal of emotion in the country due to the importance of the issue and the need to take measures to protect women and children from sexual and gender-based violence. The proposed law on sexual and gender-based violence against women and children is an important step towards a safer and more equitable society for all.
