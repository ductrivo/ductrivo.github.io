### Weather: return of the heatwave, heavy atmosphere, thunderstorms, what's the weather forecast for this week?


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/1b413e63-7adb-40c3-b065-3badf00670e3/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Weather: return of the heatwave, heavy atmosphere, thunderstorms, what's the weather forecast for this week?"
    caption="Météo : retour de la canicule, atmosphère lourde, orages, quel temps pour cette semaine ?" %}


The next week in France will be marked by a return of heatwaves, with a heavy atmosphere and the possibility of thunderstorms. Temperatures will rise from Tuesday onwards, with a risk of thunderstorms on Thursday and Friday. According to Météo France, records of temperatures could be broken. Here are the detailed forecasts:

- Monday: pleasant weather, with an average temperature of 28°C in the south and 23°C in the north.
- Tuesday: rising temperatures, with an average temperature of 30°C in the south and 25°C in the north.
- Wednesday: temperatures continue to rise, with an average temperature of 31°C in the south and 26°C in the north.
- Thursday: risk of thunderstorms, with an average temperature of 32°C in the south and 27°C in the north.
- Friday: thunderstorms may occur again, with an average temperature of 32°C in the south and 27°C in the north.
- Saturday: the heat persists, with an average temperature of 31°C in the south and 26°C in the north.
- Sunday: temperatures begin to fall slightly, with an average temperature of 30°C in the south and 25°C in the north.
