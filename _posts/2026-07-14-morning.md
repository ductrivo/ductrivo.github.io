### 'They spend days hiding in shelters': how Russian soldiers disguised as Ukrainian civilians are infiltrating the Donbass.


Russian soldiers were disguising themselves as Ukrainian civilians to infiltrate the Donbass region clandestinely, according to a report by the Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE). According to information gathered by OSCE observers, these soldiers would move in groups with Ukrainian civilians to avoid Ukrainian military checks. The Russian soldiers were identified by their military equipment and their different languages from those of the local civilians. The OSCE also reported that these infiltrations took place in several cities in the Donbass, including Debaltseve, Horlivka and Yenakievo. This practice would violate the Minsk agreements, which provide for the withdrawal of foreign troops from the Ukrainian territory.
