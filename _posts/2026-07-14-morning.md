### Our tips for visiting the 'Provençal Alps' this summer: Laragne market, Gorges de la Méouge, and water sports areas.


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/20243f98-988f-4045-b557-beda44024c9b/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Our tips for visiting the 'Provençal Alps' this summer: Laragne market, Gorges de la Méouge, and water sports areas."
    caption="Nos conseils pour visiter les 'Alpes provençales' cet été : marché de Laragne, Gorges de la Méouge et plans d'eau" %}


The Provence Alps offer an unforgettable experience for summer vacations. Here are some tips for making the most of this region:

1. The market in Laragne is a great place to discover local products and Provençal culinary traditions. It takes place every Wednesday and Saturday morning.
2. The Gorges de la Méouge are a spectacular natural attraction that should not be missed. They offer stunning views of the surrounding mountains and are perfect for hiking or mountain biking.
3. The lakes in the region are ideal for swimming and water sports. Lake Serre-Ponçon is the largest and offers a beach, restaurants, and various activities.
4. Between charming villages such as Sisteron, Buëch, and Montclus, you can discover the history and culture of the region. The Roman ruins of Glanum are particularly interesting to visit.
5. The region also offers many outdoor activities, such as rafting, canyoning, and climbing. It is possible to organize guided excursions to discover the best areas.
6. Finally, the Durance River offers a unique opportunity to swim in nature. It flows through the Verdon Regional Natural Park and offers spectacular views of the surrounding rocky cliffs.

In summary, the Sisteron-Buëch area is a region rich in activities and tourist attractions for enjoying the summer in the Provence Alps.

### Wombat: Why this amazing marsupial makes cube-shaped droppings


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/63eee7c2-0379-400b-a9d5-eb99daf61906/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="Wombat: Why this amazing marsupial makes cube-shaped droppings"
    caption="Wombat : pourquoi cet étonnant marsupial fait des crottes en forme de cube" %}


The wombat is an Australian marsupial notable for its unique characteristics. It is known for its cube-shaped droppings, a feature that makes it the subject of curiosity and scientific research. Indeed, wombats have developed this habit to minimize the degradation of their habitat by insects when they are hiding in burrows.

The wombat also has a rear that is used as a shield, which allows it to effectively protect itself from predators. With a weight that can reach up to 35 kilograms and a length of over one meter, it is a large animal in its ecosystem.

The wombat plays an essential role in its ecosystem by helping to regulate the populations of vegetation and contributing to biodiversity. It is exhibited at the ZooParc de Beauval, where visitors can discover this rare animal and admire its unique characteristics.
