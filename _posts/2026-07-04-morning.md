### VIDEO - 'I design the garden a bit like a painting.' Discovering the wonderful garden of Odile


{% include figure.html
    src="https://www.ici.fr/pikapi/images/2bc1e94a-6c08-4cfe-b28b-ff073f8f45f8/920x517?webp=false"
    alt="VIDEO - 'I design the garden a bit like a painting.' Discovering the wonderful garden of Odile"
    caption="VIDÉO - 'Je conçois le jardin un peu comme un tableau'. A la découverte du merveilleux jardin d'Odile" %}


Odile's Garden, located in Saint-Cosme-en-Vairais, is a green space created by the designers Odile and Gérard Delorme. This garden, described as a painting, offers an oasis of tranquility and natural beauty.

The garden covers more than 10 hectares and is composed of many different areas: flower meadows, forests, ponds, paths lined with century-old trees, as well as a tropical greenhouse. Visitors can also discover an exceptional collection of exotic and rare plants.

The garden was designed to offer a space for rest and contemplation to its visitors. The winding paths, panoramic views, and the richness of colors make Odile's Garden a unique place to relax and escape from everyday life.

The garden is open to the public year-round, but the seasons change the way it appears. The spring flowers, the autumn foliage, and the winter snowdrops offer each a unique experience of the garden.

Odile's Garden is a place where nature and art combine to create an exceptionally beautiful and peaceful space. It is recommended for all nature lovers who are looking for an oasis of peace in the heart of the French countryside.
