Fire rages in the Pyrénées-Orientales: over 10,000 people evacuated

Since Saturday, a major fire has been raging in the Ille-sur-Têt area (Pyrénées-Orientales). The flames have already covered more than 1650 hectares. A total of 700 firefighters are on the scene to control the fire.

Residents of the Aspres massif and those of Ille-sur-Têt have been evacuated, representing over 10,000 people. Authorities are urging citizens to stay informed about the situation via local media.
