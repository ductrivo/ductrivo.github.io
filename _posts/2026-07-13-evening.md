### SNCF Delays: What is the Pegasus plan, launched on Monday after the fires?


The Pégase plan is an emergency program implemented by the Société Nationale des Chemins de Fer Français (SNCF) in the wake of recent fires. Its objective is to restore normal rail service in affected areas as quickly as possible, while ensuring the safety of passengers and equipment. The plan includes several measures, such as increasing the number of trains, using additional locomotives, relocating personnel and equipment to affected areas, and prioritizing the repair of damaged infrastructure. Passengers are also informed in real-time about delays and changes to rail services via various communication channels. The Pégase plan is an important tool for restoring normal rail service after the fires, while ensuring the safety of passengers and equipment.

### 'Patriotism yes, nationalism never': Macron's warning against the RN


"Emmanuel Macron clearly warned against the National Rally (RN), stating: "Patriotism, yes; nationalism, never." This statement refers to the French president's political position towards this far-right French political party. Macron emphasizes that patriotism involves an emotional and moral attachment to one's homeland, while nationalism is considered a dangerous form of ethnocentric and xenophobic extremism. This political position of Macron was highlighted during the 2017 French presidential election, where he opposed the RN and its leader, Marine Le Pen. Since taking office, Macron has continued to criticize nationalism and promote a more inclusive and open vision of patriotism.
