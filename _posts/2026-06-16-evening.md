    caption="Drôme. « Des nettoyeurs de la nature » : pourquoi les vautours sont de retour dans les Baronnies" %}


The Drôme department is witnessing the return of vultures in the Baronnies thanks to the Vautours en Baronnies association. For 30 years, this association has been carrying out a project to reintroduce the four endemic vulture species of Western Europe into the regional natural park of the Baronnies Provençales. Last May, two young griffon vultures were released on cliffs. This species primarily feeds on bones and is one of the most widespread in Europe. This action aims to restore local biodiversity and promote ecological balance in the region.
